About Us

WHAT DRIVES US

Why we do what we do.

 

We are a world class organization who's aim is to increase the number of smiles around the world by linking people with what they think is missing in their lives;in this case companions.

OUR AIM

What we want to achieve.

We would like to have a world where people are happy and stress free because we are fighting to defeat depression,anxiety and other mental illnesses.
In our world today,many people suffer in silence because they have no one to talk to or keep them company. Some are unable to talk to their friends or family because they fear how they might see them. As much as animals can't speak,they'll be there to make you feel better and lift your spirits.

WHEN WE STARTED

Our organization was started in November 2019.

WHO STARTED IT

It was started by Gabriel Ojiambo when he adopted a stray kitten.

OUR MISSION

To have a world free from mental illness by spreading smiles.

OUR VISION

OUR GOAL

To create a world where laughter is the norm.
